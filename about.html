How It Works

Simply enter your monthly take-home income, select your household type (single or two people), choose your bedroom preference, and instantly see which cities are most affordable for your situation. You can search, filter, and sort by cost, percentage of income, or geographic region.

Our intuitive interface lets you compare cities side-by-side, helping you make informed decisions about relocation, remote work opportunities, or your next adventure abroad.

Our Data

Cost of living data is aggregated from multiple public sources and represents average costs for each city. While we strive for accuracy, actual costs can vary significantly based on neighborhood, lifestyle, and current economic conditions.

This tool is intended as a starting point for research, not as definitive financial advice. We recommend using our calculator alongside other resources when planning your move or evaluating new locations.
