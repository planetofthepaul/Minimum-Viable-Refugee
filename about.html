About Minimum Viable Expat

Minimum Viable Expat is a free tool that helps you understand how livable different cities are based on your personal income situation. Whether you're a digital nomad, expat, remote worker, or simply considering relocation, our calculator provides instant clarity on what percentage of your income would go toward living expenses in 170+ cities worldwide.

What We Do

We've compiled cost of living data for over 170 cities across every continent, including:

  • Monthly rent for 1-bedroom and 2-bedroom apartments
  • Estimated living expenses for singles and couples
  • Breakdown of housing, food, utilities, transit, and other costs
  • Real-time calculation of affordability based on your income

How It Works

Simply enter your monthly take-home income, select your household type (single or two people), choose your bedroom preference, and instantly see which cities are most affordable for your situation. You can search, filter, and sort by cost, percentage of income, or geographic region.

Our Data

Cost of living data is aggregated from multiple public sources and represents average costs for each city. While we strive for accuracy, actual costs can vary significantly based on neighborhood, lifestyle, and current economic conditions. This tool is intended as a starting point for research, not as definitive financial advice.

Why "Minimum Viable Expat"?

The name reflects the reality that many people today—whether seeking economic opportunity, searching for a better quality of life, or exploring new horizons—need practical tools to make informed decisions about where to live. This tool aims to democratize access to cost-of-living information.
